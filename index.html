ABOUT US

We Provide The Best Food For You!

Kamayan catering services offers a truly unique and immersive dining experience that brings the authentic flavors and traditions of Filipino cuisine to your special event or gathering. Whether it's a wedding, a birthday celebration, a corporate event, or any other occasion, kamayan catering adds a touch of cultural richness and culinary delight that will leave your guests amazed. The kamayan catering team is experienced and knowledgeable in Filipino cuisine and culture, ensuring that the dishes are prepared with authenticity and attention to detail. They use high-quality, locally- sourced ingredients to create flavorful and visually appealing dishes that cater to different dietary preferences and requirements. It opened in 2019, the modern restaurant offers unforgettable culinary experiences with fresh, diverse, and creatively plated dishes. Join us for exceptional service and a unique dining experience!

WHAT WE OFFER

Buttered Garlic Mixed Seafood

It is a flavorful and aromatic dish, combination of different types of seafood, such as shrimp, mussels, and squid, cooked in a buttery garlic sauce.

Buffalo Wings

It is a deep-fried chicken wings and then coated in a spicy and tangy sauce made with a combination of hot sauce, butter, and other seasonings.

Lumpia

Mixture of vegetables, such as shredded carrots and bean sprouts, and minced meat, such as pork or chicken that is tightly in a thin pastry wrapper.

Pork chop

It is meat dish in many cuisines around the world, a relatively thick slice of pan-fryed meat,that is seasoned with a variety of herbs and spices.
